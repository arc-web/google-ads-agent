## Search Theme Strategy

### Local Intent Themes
- Include "near me" variations

- Focus on service area targeting

### Service-Specific Themes
- Treatment-specific search terms
- Procedure names

### Brand Themes
- Brand name variations

- Search themes guide Performance Max AI targeting
- Themes should align with actual services offered
