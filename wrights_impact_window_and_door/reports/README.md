### Key Metrics to Calculate
```
Efficiency Metrics:
├── CTR = Clicks ÷ Impressions
├── CPC = Cost ÷ Clicks
├── CPA = Cost ÷ Conversions
└── ROAS = Revenue ÷ Cost

Performance Metrics:
├── Conversion Rate = Conversions ÷ Clicks
├── Quality Score = (CTR × Landing Page × Ad Relevance) ÷ 3
├── Impression Share = Impressions ÷ Total Available Impressions
└── Geographic ROI = Revenue ÷ Geographic Cost
```
