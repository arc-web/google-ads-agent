
# AD SCHEDULING STRATEGY - Wright's Impact Window and Door

## Purpose
Document dayparting and seasonal scheduling approaches for Wright's local service business optimization.

## Core Business Hours Strategy

### Primary Business Hours: Monday-Saturday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
**Rationale**: Standard contractor business hours, peak home improvement decision time
**Implementation**:
- **Monday-Friday**: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (10 hours)
- **Saturday**: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM (8 hours)
- **Sunday**: No scheduling (office closed)

## Emergency Scheduling Protocols

### Hurricane Watch (48 Hours Notice)
**Immediate Action**:
- Extend hours to 6:00 AM - 9:00 PM daily
- Increase bid modifiers by +50%
- Enable Sunday scheduling if needed
- Activate emergency messaging

### Hurricane Warning (24 Hours Notice)
**Immediate Action**:
- 24/7 scheduling activation
- Maximum bid modifiers (+100%)
- Emergency consultation focus
- Coordinate with local authorities

### Post-Storm Response (Immediate Aftermath)
**Action**:
- Extended weekend hours for assessments
- Emergency bid adjustments
- Focus on storm damage services
- Coordinate with insurance companies

## Performance-Based Scheduling Adjustments

### Low-Performing Hours Reduction
**Trigger**: CPA 50% above average for specific hour blocks
**Action**: Reduce or eliminate scheduling for underperforming hours
**Example**: If 5-6 PM shows poor performance, end day at 5:00 PM

### High-Performing Hours Expansion
**Trigger**: Conversion rate 25% above average for specific hours
**Action**: Extend scheduling into high-performing time blocks
**Example**: If morning hours excel, start at 7:00 AM instead of 8:00 AM

## Geographic Time Zone Considerations

### Lee County (EST - No Adjustment)
**Rationale**: Primary service area matches business timezone
**Strategy**: Standard scheduling applies

### Broward County (Also EST)
