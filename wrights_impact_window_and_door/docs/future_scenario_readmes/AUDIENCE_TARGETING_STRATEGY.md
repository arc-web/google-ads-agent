
# AUDIENCE TARGETING STRATEGY - Wright's Impact Window and Door

## Purpose
Document audience segment selection and optimization approaches for Wright's local service business targeting homeowners and property owners in hurricane-prone areas.

## Core Target Audience Profile

### Primary Audience: Homeowners in Hurricane Zones
**Demographics**:
- Age: 35-65 (peak home improvement spending)
- Income: $75,000+ (can afford impact installations)
- Homeowners: Primary target (vs. renters)
- Geographic: Lee and Broward counties, FL

**Psychographics**:
- Risk-aware (hurricane preparedness)
- Property-focused (home value protection)
- Local service preference ("near me" searches)
- Insurance-conscious (insurance discount motivation)

## Google Audience Targeting Implementation

### Demographic Targeting

#### Age Targeting: 35-65
**Rationale**: Peak earning years, established homeowners, family formation
**Implementation**:
- Primary: 35-44, 45-54, 55-64
- Secondary: 25-34, 65+ (as appropriate for campaigns)
- Exclusion: Under 25 (typically renters, limited budget)

#### Income Targeting: $75,000+
**Rationale**: Minimum income threshold for impact window installations
**Implementation**:
- Target: $75,000 - $100,000, $100,000+
- Higher modifiers for $150,000+ segments
- Monitor conversion rates by income bracket

#### Gender Targeting: Balanced Approach
**Rationale**: Home improvement decisions involve both partners
**Implementation**:
- No gender restrictions (allow both)
- Monitor performance by gender for insights
- Adjust messaging based on audience response

### Interest-Based Audience Targeting

#### Home Improvement Enthusiasts
**Google Audience Segments**:
- "Home improvement" interest category
- "Home renovation" interest category
- "DIY home improvement" interest category
**Rationale**: Active home improvement researchers

#### Hurricane Preparedness
**Custom Audience Strategy**:
- Target search terms: "hurricane shutters," "storm protection"
- Remarketing: Visitors to hurricane preparedness sites
- Lookalike: Customers who purchased storm protection
**Rationale**: High-intent hurricane preparation searchers

#### Property Insurance Focused
**Google Audience Segments**:
- "Home insurance" interest category

- Remarketing: Insurance quote site visitors
**Rationale**: Insurance-driven impact window decisions

### Life Events Targeting

#### New Homeowners (0-2 years)
**Rationale**: Immediate need for impact protection in new purchases
**Implementation**:
- Target "recent home buyers" audience segment
- Combine with new construction area targeting
- Highest priority audience (50% bid boost)

#### Home Renovators (Active Projects)
**Rationale**: Current home improvement projects create opportunities
**Implementation**:
- Target "home renovation" life event segment
- Focus on kitchen/bathroom renovation audiences
- Medium priority (25% bid boost)

### Custom Audience Segments

#### Past Customers (Remarketing)
**Implementation**:
- Website visitors (30 days)
- Phone call initiators (remarketing lists)
- Quote requestors (highest value audience)
**Strategy**:
- 90-day cookie window
- Cross-device targeting enabled
- Frequency capping: 5 impressions/day

#### Competitor Site Visitors
**Implementation**:
- Target visitors to competing impact window sites
- Remarketing with competitive positioning
- Focus on "why choose Wright's" messaging
**Legal Note**: Ensure compliance with Google policies

#### Geographic Intent Signals
**Implementation**:
- Target users searching Lee/Broward specific terms
- Local intent keywords: "Fort Myers impact windows"
- Proximity-based audience signals
**Strategy**: Combine with ZIP code targeting

**Tier 1 - High Intent (0-7 days)**:
- Visitors who requested quotes
- Phone number submissions
- Project consultation bookings

**Tier 2 - Medium Intent (8-30 days)**:
- General website visitors
- Service page viewers
- Blog post readers
- Bid modifier: +50%

**Tier 3 - Low Intent (31-90 days)**:

## Lookalike Audience Strategy

### Customer Base Expansion
**Source Audience**: Past customers who purchased impact windows
**Lookalike Creation**:
- 1% similar: High confidence, direct competitors
- 2-5% similar: Balanced expansion, primary target
- 10% similar: Broad expansion, test audience

**Performance Monitoring**:
- Compare lookalike CPA vs. source audience CPA
- Monitor audience size and reach
- Adjust similarity percentage based on results
