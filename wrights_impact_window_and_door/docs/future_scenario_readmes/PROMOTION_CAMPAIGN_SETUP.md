## Campaign Structure for Promotion Campaigns

### Dedicated Promotion Campaign
**Strategy**: Separate campaign for promotional traffic
**Rationale**: Isolate promotional performance from organic campaigns
**Budget**: 30-50% of regular campaign budget during promotion

### Promotion Integration with Regular Campaigns
**Strategy**: Add promotion extensions to existing campaigns
**Rationale**: Leverage existing traffic for promotional messaging
**Implementation**: Enable promotion extensions across all active campaigns

## Bid Strategy for Promotion Campaigns

### Aggressive Bidding During Promotions
**Target CPA**: 20-30% below normal CPA targets
**Rationale**: Promotions drive higher-intent traffic
**Implementation**:
- Increase bid modifiers by +25% during promotion
- Use Target CPA bidding with aggressive targets
- Monitor conversion volume vs. cost

### Seasonal Bid Adjustments
****Slow Season**: +75% bid boost for acquisition promotions
**Rationale**: Maximize promotion visibility during key periods

## Audience Targeting for Promotions

### New Customer Focus
**Primary Audience**: Non-remarketing audiences
**Strategy**: Target users who haven't visited the site
**Implementation**: Exclude remarketing audiences from promotion campaigns

### High-Intent Segments
**Target Audiences**:
- Recent home buyers
- Home improvement searchers
- Insurance quote seekers
- Hurricane preparedness researchers

### Geographic Precision
**ZIP Code Targeting**: Focus on high-value promotion areas
**Radius Targeting**: 25-mile radius from office locations
**Strategy**: Maximize local response to time-sensitive offers
