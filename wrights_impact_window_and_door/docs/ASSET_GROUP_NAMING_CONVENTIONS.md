## GOOGLE ADS EDITOR COMPATIBILITY

### Supported Characters:
- Letters (a-z, A-Z)
- Numbers (0-9)
- Underscores (_)
- Hyphens (-) - but avoid for consistency

### Avoid:
- Spaces
- Special characters (!@#$%^&*())
- Accented characters (unless necessary)
- Emojis or symbols
