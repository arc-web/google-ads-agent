
# Wright's Impact Window and Door Performance Max Campaign Documentation

## Campaign Overview
Wright's Impact Window and Door Performance Max campaigns focus on impact windows and doors in Lee County and Broward County, Florida. This campaign structure emphasizes service-specific + regional targeting with hyper-local city-level "near me" asset groups.

## Campaign Structure

### Campaign Naming Convention
- **wrights_[county]_pmax**: Wright's prefix + county name + pmax suffix
- Example: `wrights_lee_county_pmax`, `wrights_broward_county_pmax`

### Asset Group Structure
Each campaign contains two types of asset groups:

#### 1. Service + Regional Asset Groups
- **impact_windows_regional**: Impact windows targeting across county
- **impact_doors_regional**: Impact doors targeting across county
- **hurricane_protection_regional**: Hurricane protection services across county
- **energy_efficiency_regional**: Energy efficiency solutions across county
- **commercial_solutions_regional**: Commercial property solutions across county

#### 2. City-Specific Near Me Asset Groups
- **[city_name]_near_me**: City-specific "near me" targeting
- Examples: `fort_myers_near_me`, `fort_lauderdale_near_me`

## Geographic Targeting Strategy

### Lee County Campaign (wrights_lee_county_pmax)
**Service Groups**: County-wide targeting for Lee County, FL
**City Groups**:
- Fort Myers (Primary business hub, 58 ZIP codes)
- Cape Coral (High-growth residential, 7 ZIP codes)
- Naples (Luxury market, 16 ZIP codes)

### Broward County Campaign (wrights_broward_county_pmax)
**Service Groups**: County-wide targeting for Broward County, FL
**City Groups**:
- Fort Lauderdale (Downtown business district, 46 ZIP codes)
- Pompano Beach (Beach communities, 15 ZIP codes)
- Hollywood (Residential communities, 13 ZIP codes)

## Keyword Strategy

### Service + Regional Keywords
Focus on service-specific terms without generic "services":
- Impact windows, hurricane windows, storm windows
- Impact doors, hurricane doors, storm doors
- Hurricane protection, storm protection, impact protection
- Energy efficient windows, energy savings, utility bill reduction
- Commercial impact windows, commercial hurricane protection

### City Near Me Keywords
Hyper-local targeting for each city:
- "[service] [city name] near me"
- Example: "impact windows fort myers near me"
- No generic "impact windows near me" - all city-specific

## Performance Max Implementation

### Asset Requirements
- **Product Images**: High-quality photos of impact windows/doors
- **Service Benefit Images**: Energy savings, storm protection visuals
- **Local Area Photos**: City-specific imagery where possible
- **Testimonials**: Customer success stories
- **Call-to-Action**: "Get Free Quote", "Schedule Consultation"

### Geographic Targeting Setup
- **Service Groups**: County-level targeting only
- **City Groups**: Specific city + ZIP code targeting
- **No Radius Targeting**: Explicit geographic boundaries only

## Budget Strategy
- **Initial Budget**: $500/day per campaign
- **Scaling Target**: $1,000-2,000/day based on performance
- **Geographic Split**: 40% Lee County, 60% Broward County (population-based)

## Success Metrics
- **Primary**: Cost per acquisition, return on ad spend
- **Secondary**: Geographic attribution accuracy, asset group performance
- **Tertiary**: Search term coverage, conversion quality

## Technical Notes
- **Format**: Performance Max (asset group-based, not keyword-based)
- **No Ad Groups**: Performance Max uses asset groups, not traditional ad groups
- **Geographic Precision**: City + ZIP code level targeting for near me groups
- **Service Specificity**: No generic services - all targeting is service + location specific

## Risk Mitigation
- Conservative initial budgets
- Direct implementation approach
- Geographic performance monitoring
- Asset approval validation before scaling
- Rollback plan for underperforming campaigns
