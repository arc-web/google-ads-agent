
# Wright's Impact Window and Door Performance Max Campaign Documentation

## Campaign Overview - CORRECTED APPROACH
Wright's Impact Window and Door campaigns use a **LOCAL SERVICE BUSINESS** approach with **NO BROAD TARGETING**. All asset groups are hyper-local **Service + Near Me** targeting only.

## Campaign Structure - SERVICE + NEAR ME ONLY

### Campaign Naming Convention
- **wrights_[county]_pmax**: Wright's prefix + county name + pmax suffix
- Example: `wrights_lee_county_pmax`, `wrights_broward_county_pmax`

### Asset Group Structure - NO BROAD TARGETING
**REMOVED**: All broad/county-wide service groups
**KEPT**: Only hyper-local Service + Near Me asset groups

#### Service + Near Me Asset Groups (ONLY)
Each city has 5 service-specific asset groups:
- **impact_windows_[city]_near_me**: Impact windows targeting for specific city
- **impact_doors_[city]_near_me**: Impact doors targeting for specific city  
- **hurricane_protection_[city]_near_me**: Hurricane protection for specific city
- **energy_efficiency_[city]_near_me**: Energy efficiency for specific city
- **commercial_solutions_[city]_near_me**: Commercial solutions for specific city
