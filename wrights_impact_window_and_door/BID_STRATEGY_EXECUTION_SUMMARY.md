## The New Bid Strategy Framework

### Core Principles Implemented

#### 1. **Linear Progression, Not Chaos**
```
6 HUMAN-MANAGEABLE TIERS:
$1.25 → $1.50 → $1.75 → $2.00 → $2.25 → $2.50

Each tier represents:
- Service profitability
- Competitive intensity
- Geographic importance
- Business priority
```

#### 2. **Single Strategy Per Campaign Type**
```
Search Campaigns: Manual CPC
├── Why: Control + precision optimization
├── Result: Predictable costs, strategic adjustments
└── Management: Human oversight with automation support

Performance Max: Target ROAS 400%
├── Why: Revenue optimization across networks
├── Result: 4:1 return on ad spend
└── Management: Automated with human monitoring

Local Campaigns: Maximize Conversions
├── Why: Proximity intent drives high conversion rates
├── Result: Volume-focused for "near me" searches
└── Management: Budget control with conversion goals
```

#### 3. **Business Logic-Based Tiers**
```
SERVICE VALUE TIERS:
├── Commercial Solutions: $2.50 (highest lifetime value)
├── Hurricane Protection: $2.25 (emergency priority)
├── Impact Windows: $1.75-$2.00 (core business)
├── Impact Doors: $1.50-$1.75 (secondary service)
└── Energy Efficiency: $1.25-$1.50 (consultation driver)

GEOGRAPHIC PRIORITY MULTIPLIERS:
├── Primary Cities (Fort Myers, Naples, Fort Lauderdale): +0.25 tier
├── Growth Markets (Cape Coral): Baseline tiers
└── Luxury Segments (Naples premium homes): +0.25 tier
```
