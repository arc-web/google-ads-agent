# EvoRestore National Campaign Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the national-level campaign strategy for EvoRestore.com, focusing on Performance Max campaigns that can scale across multiple service areas while maintaining local relevance and emergency response positioning.

## Strategic Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. **National Brand Awareness**: Establish EvoRestore as a trusted restoration expert
2. **Lead Generation**: Drive qualified leads through multiple channels
3. **Emergency Response Positioning**: 24/7 availability and fast response messaging
4. **Local Service Dominance**: Geo-targeted campaigns for service area control

### Key Performance Indicators
- **Cost per Acquisition**: <$40
- **Conversion Rate**: >3%
- **Return on Ad Spend**: >300%
- **Phone Call Share**: >25% of total conversions
- **Form Submission Rate**: >2%

## Geographic Targeting Strategy

### Primary Service Areas
- **Radius Targeting**: 25-50 mile radius from service locations
- **City Targeting**: High-value cities and zip codes
- **Regional Campaigns**: State-wide coverage where applicable
- **DMA Targeting**: Designated Market Areas for broader reach

### Location-Based Asset Groups
- **Local Headlines**: Include city names in headlines
- **Local Sitelinks**: Service area specific landing pages
- **Local Callouts**: "Serving [City] Area"
- **Local Extensions**: Business location and directions

## Budget Optimization Strategy

### Daily Budget Allocation
- **Emergency Services**: 35% ($150-200)
- **Water Damage**: 25% ($100-150)
- **Fire Damage**: 20% ($80-120)
- **Mold Remediation**: 10% ($40-60)
- **Storm Damage**: 10% ($40-60)

### Seasonal Adjustments
- **Storm Season**: +50% to Emergency and Storm Damage
- **Winter Months**: +25% to Water Damage and Mold
- **Summer Months**: Balanced distribution
- **Holiday Periods**: Reduced budgets for planning

### Performance-Based Scaling
- **ROAS >400%**: Increase budget by 25%
- **ROAS 300-400%**: Maintain current budget
- **ROAS 200-300%**: 10% budget increase test
- **ROAS <200%**: Budget decrease and optimization
