Campaign Structure

The launch starts compact. Future campaigns can split by service or region only after the data shows where separate budgets and reporting would help.

Launch shape

The current launch uses 1 controlled Search campaign with 8 service-based ad groups. This keeps early learning clean and easier to review.

Budget

Why Spend Increases As Performance Gets Clearer

This page explains the operating logic behind the pacing curve. We do not spend more because the campaign is guessing. We spend more when the evidence is cleaner.

Why spend increases as performance gets clearer

Early spend is intentionally cautious. As waste is cut and signals improve, more of the same monthly budget can be committed with confidence.

StepWhat improves
AWe see which searches are worth paying for before scaling.
BLow-value traffic is trimmed.
CCPC, conversions, and lead quality become easier to read.
DWe can raise spend because the data behind it is cleaner.

What this means for your campaign

We pace the full month

Daily spend and clicks will move. The aim is healthy pacing through day 30.

Starting lighter protects budget

Early limits leave room to learn before we push winning areas.

CPC changes click volume

The same dollars buy more or fewer clicks as auctions shift.

Later spend is intentional

Increases follow clearer performance, not random day-to-day noise.
