Strategy

A Controlled New Search Campaign

This is the first version of the campaign plan. It keeps the launch focused on people already looking for therapy support, then gives you a clear way to approve services, priorities, copy, and regions before spend begins.

What we are proposing

Search-first launch

The campaign is built for people already searching for therapy support, not broad awareness. All keywords use phrase match for cleaner early data.

Website-backed services

The launch focuses on services visible on the site: Anxiety Therapy, Individual Therapy, Group Therapy, CBT Therapy, DBT Therapy, OCD Therapy, Depression Therapy, Social Anxiety Therapy.

Priority still needs input

Confirm which services should receive the most traffic before the launch budget is finalized.

Launch review status

The CSV validates cleanly, but remains paused until budget, service priority, and your approval decisions are confirmed.

Audience and service area

Audience from source material: adolescents, young adults in their 20s and 30s, adults for group therapy

Positioning: online therapy for anxiety and related concerns

Targeting: New York, United States, New Jersey, United States

Targeting method: Location of presence

Why Spend Increases As Performance Gets Clearer

This page explains the operating logic behind the pacing curve. We do not spend more because the campaign is guessing. We spend more when the evidence is cleaner.

Why spend increases as performance gets clearer

Early spend is intentionally cautious. As waste is cut and signals improve, more of the same monthly budget can be committed with confidence.

StepWhat improves
AWe see which searches are worth paying for before scaling.
BLow-value traffic is trimmed.
CCPC, conversions, and lead quality become easier to read.
DWe can raise spend because the data behind it is cleaner.

What this means for your campaign

We pace the full month

Daily spend and clicks will move. The aim is healthy pacing through day 30.

Starting lighter protects budget

Early limits leave room to learn before we push winning areas.

CPC changes click volume

The same dollars buy more or fewer clicks as auctions shift.

Later spend is intentional

Increases follow clearer performance, not random day-to-day noise.
