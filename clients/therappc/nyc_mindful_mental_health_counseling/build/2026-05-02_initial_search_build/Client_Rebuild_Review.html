Campaign Structure

How The Campaign Can Grow Over Time

The launch starts compact. Future campaigns can split by service or region only after the data shows where separate budgets and reporting would help.

Launch shape

The current launch uses 2 controlled Search campaigns with 42 service and geo intent ad groups. This keeps early learning clean and easier to review.

Expansion rule

City ad groups should be added only when approved city priorities are supplied or performance data shows a region needs its own copy and reporting focus.

One campaign now

The first launch stays compact so the early budget is not spread too thin.

Split later by proof

Future campaign splits should follow lead quality, conversion signals, and search demand.

No extra campaigns yet

The service and regional splits shown here are future paths, not active launch campaigns.
