Blocking Revisions

Facts We Must Correct Before Launch

01

Insurance language

Only Anthem/CareFirst BCBS is in-network. Other carriers are handled through out-of-network superbills.

Pre-launch required Replace broad insurance claims with client-approved language.

02

EMDR capacity

No current clinician openings for EMDR.

Pre-launch required Remove EMDR from active copy, active keywords, and roadmap.

03

ADHD testing age limit

ADHD testing is available through age 21 only.

Pre-launch required Reposition as ADHD Testing for Children, Teens, and Young Adults. Add adult-intent negatives.

04

Parent-child services versus standalone family therapy

Parent-child therapy and family support are available around an identified child patient. Standalone family therapy is not offered.

Pre-launch required Replace family therapy language with parent-child therapy or family support language. Add family therapy negatives.

05

Bidding, geo modifiers, and conversion tracking

Client needs clarity on bid strategy, geo modifiers, conversion tracking, PHI handling, GA4, and Curve Compliance.

Pre-launch required Prepare launch-readiness explanation and verify no PHI is passed to Google measurement systems.

06

Virginia LLC to PLLC conversion

Legal entity name will change in the next month or two and may affect healthcare advertiser verification.

Pre-launch required Coordinate verification timing before launch and avoid unnecessary mid-flight verification changes.

Strategic Revisions

Priority, Budget, Brand, and Geo Decisions

Psychiatric NP referral growth target
Psychiatric NP referrals are the highest growth priority. Target is 15 to 20 referrals per week.
Revision focus: Consider a psychiatry-focused campaign split and heavier budget weighting instead of simply adding more ad groups.
Brand campaign on day 1
Client wants to discuss launching brand campaign immediately with modest monthly budget.
Revision focus: Approve if budget is capped and brand reporting stays separate from nonbrand.
Maryland exclusion
Client believes Maryland should likely be excluded due to CPA, logistics, and telehealth limits.
Revision focus: Do not use old CPA alone as the proof. Decide based on licensing, telehealth, commute friction, landing page support, and budget test appetite.
Decision Logic

How these items should affect launch

Change launch weighting

  • Psychiatry becomes the primary growth focus because the client needs psychiatric NP referral volume.
  • Brand launches separately with a capped budget so brand and nonbrand metrics stay clean.
  • Testing and adult therapy remain active, but they should not crowd out psychiatry allocation.

Do not blindly rebuild architecture

  • Maryland should be excluded or isolated based on logistics, telehealth constraints, and test appetite.
  • Provider-name brand coverage is optional and should be kept low-budget until search volume proves value.
  • Strategic changes become CSV changes only after client approval.
