# Video Data Reports

This directory contains video asset performance and YouTube campaign data for video advertising analysis.

## 📄 Video report.csv

### What This File Represents
**Video Asset Performance Data** - Comprehensive metrics for video ads across YouTube and other video platforms

### Key Data Included
- **Video Assets**: Individual video performance metrics and metadata
- **View Metrics**: Impressions, views, view rate, completion rates
- **Engagement Data**: Likes, shares, comments, subscriber gains
- **Cost Data**: CPM, CPVV (cost per view), total video costs
- **Audience Demographics**: Age, gender, geographic distribution of viewers
- **Video Performance**: Watch time, quartiles (25%, 50%, 75%, 100% completion)

### Primary Use Cases
- **Video Creative Optimization**: Identify top-performing video content
- **Audience Engagement Analysis**: Understand viewer behavior and preferences
- **Cost Efficiency**: Compare video advertising ROI against other formats
- **Content Strategy**: Determine which video types resonate with target audience
- **Platform Optimization**: Optimize YouTube campaign performance

### Key Columns to Analyze
- **Video ID/Title**: Individual video asset identification
- **View Metrics**: Views, view rate, watch time, completion rates
- **Engagement Metrics**: Likes, shares, comments, click-through rates
- **Cost Metrics**: Total cost, CPM, CPVV, cost per completed view
- **Audience Data**: Demographics and geographic distribution
- **Performance Status**: Video approval status and serving restrictions

### Strategic Insights Available
- **Content Effectiveness**: Which video topics/styles drive engagement
- **Audience Resonance**: Demographic analysis of engaged viewers
- **Cost Efficiency**: Video advertising ROI vs. other creative formats
- **Creative Optimization**: Video length, format, and messaging effectiveness
- **Platform Performance**: YouTube campaign effectiveness and optimization opportunities

### Analysis Recommendations

#### Video Performance Analysis
1. **View Rate Optimization**: Identify videos with highest view-through rates
2. **Engagement Correlation**: Analyze relationship between engagement and conversions
3. **Cost Efficiency**: Compare CPVV across different video types and lengths
4. **Audience Targeting**: Optimize demographic and interest-based targeting

#### Creative Strategy Development
1. **Content Themes**: Identify highest-performing video topics and messaging
2. **Video Length**: Determine optimal video duration for engagement
3. **Call-to-Action**: Analyze CTA effectiveness within video content
4. **Thumbnail Performance**: Test different thumbnail designs and messaging

#### Campaign Optimization
1. **Bidding Strategy**: Optimize bids based on view rate and engagement data
2. **Audience Expansion**: Scale successful audience segments
3. **Creative Refresh**: Replace underperforming videos with high-engagement content
4. **Channel Optimization**: Optimize YouTube channel and video metadata

### Video Data Integration Strategy

#### Cross-Platform Analysis
1. **Format Comparison**: Compare video performance against static image/text ads
2. **Campaign Type Analysis**: Assess video effectiveness across Search, Display, and Discovery
3. **Geographic Performance**: Analyze video engagement by location
4. **Device Performance**: Compare desktop vs. mobile video engagement

#### Video Creative Framework
- **Hook Effectiveness**: Analyze first 5-10 seconds of video engagement
- **Message Clarity**: Measure how well video communicates key benefits
- **Brand Consistency**: Ensure video content aligns with brand messaging
- **Call-to-Action Integration**: Optimize CTAs within video content

#### Performance Forecasting
- **Content Planning**: Predict performance of new video content based on historical data
- **Budget Allocation**: Determine optimal video advertising budget based on ROI
- **Creative Testing**: A/B test video variations for continuous optimization
- **Seasonal Planning**: Plan video content calendar based on performance trends

### Video Advertising Best Practices Validation
- **View Rate Benchmarks**: Compare against industry standards (>30% target)
- **Completion Rate Goals**: Aim for >50% 25% quartile completion
- **Engagement Rate Targets**: Optimize for higher-than-average engagement
- **Cost Efficiency**: Target CPVV below $0.01 for cost-effective campaigns
