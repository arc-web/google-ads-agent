### Description Best Practices
- **Character Limit**: 90 characters maximum
- **Include**: Service + benefit + call-to-action
- **Keywords**: Match search intent
- **Local Focus**: City name in description
- **Trust Signals**: "Licensed", "Insured", "Free Estimate"

## RESPONSIVE SEARCH ADS SETUP

### Asset Groups Required:
1. **Headlines** (Min 3, Max 15, 30 chars each)
2. **Descriptions** (Min 2, Max 4, 90 chars each)
3. **Business Name**: Wright's Impact Window and Door
4. **Final URL**: Service-specific landing pages

### Headline Combinations:
Google automatically tests different combinations of your headlines and descriptions to find the best performing combinations.

## AD EXTENSIONS (CRITICAL FOR PERFORMANCE)

### 1. Location Extensions
- **Automatically Added**: Based on business location
- **Shows**: Address, phone, directions
- **Impact**: Higher CTR, local intent

### 2. Call Extensions
- **Phone Number**: Primary business number
- **Call Tracking**: Different numbers per campaign
- **Hours**: Business hours display

### 3. Sitelink Extensions

- "Insurance Approved"

## AD TESTING FRAMEWORK

### A/B Testing Structure:
1. **Control Ad**: Current best performer
2. **Test Ad**: Single variable change
3. **Test Duration**: 30 days minimum
4. **Sample Size**: 100+ clicks per ad
5. **Winner Criteria**: Statistical significance + 10% CTR improvement

### Testing Variables:
- **Headlines**: Different messaging approaches
- **Descriptions**: Benefit-focused vs. feature-focused
- **Call-to-Actions**: "Call Now" vs. "Free Estimate"
- **Local Emphasis**: City name placement

## AD PERFORMANCE MONITORING

### Daily Metrics:
- **CTR**: Target >3% for Search campaigns
- **CPC**: Monitor for efficiency
- **Conversions**: Track by ad variation
- **Quality Score**: Monitor for headline relevance

### Weekly Reviews:
- **Ad Performance Report**: Which ads perform best
- **Search Term Match**: How ads match search queries
- **Audience Response**: Different creative response rates
- **Geographic Performance**: Local ad effectiveness

### Optimization Triggers:
- **Low CTR**: <2% = creative refresh needed
- **High CPC**: >$5 = quality score or relevance issue
- **Poor Conversion**: Ad not matching landing page
- **Ad Disapproval**: Policy compliance issues
