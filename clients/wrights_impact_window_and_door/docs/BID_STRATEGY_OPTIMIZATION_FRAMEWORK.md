## Campaign-Level Strategy Standardization

### Search Campaigns: Manual CPC (Control + Optimization)

#### **Why Manual CPC for Search**
1. **Predictable Costs**: You control exact spend per click
2. **Geographic Optimization**: Easy bid adjustments by market
3. **Service Differentiation**: Different bids for different services
4. **Human Manageable**: Clear bid tiers, easy optimization

### Performance Max: Target ROAS (Results-Focused)

#### **Why Target ROAS for PMax**
1. **Automated Optimization**: Google manages bids for results
2. **Cross-Network Efficiency**: Optimizes across search, display, YouTube
3. **Conversion Focus**: Drives actions, not just clicks
4. **Scale**: Handles large keyword sets and audiences

#### **Performance Max ROAS Targets**
```
Regional PMax Campaigns:
```
