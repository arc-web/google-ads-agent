
# COMPLIANCE AND POLICY MANAGEMENT - Wright's Search Campaigns

## GOOGLE ADS POLICY REQUIREMENTS

### 1. Advertising Policies Compliance
**Required for All Ads:**
- [ ] No misleading claims
- [ ] No prohibited content (weapons, tobacco, etc.)
- [ ] No discriminatory language
- [ ] No medical claims without proper licensing
- [ ] No comparative advertising without substantiation

### 2. Editorial and Professional Requirements
**For Home Improvement Services:**
- [ ] Must have proper licensing disclosures
- [ ] Cannot make unsubstantiated claims
- [ ] Must include required disclaimers
- [ ] No false urgency or scarcity claims
- [ ] No bait-and-switch tactics

### 3. Geographic Targeting Policies
**Location-Based Advertising:**
- [ ] Cannot discriminate based on protected characteristics
- [ ] Must serve all eligible users in targeted areas
- [ ] No geographic exclusion based on demographics
- [ ] ZIP code targeting must be service-area based

## CAMPAIGN COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

### Before Upload:
- [ ] All ad text follows Google policies
- [ ] Keywords don't violate prohibited content rules
- [ ] Geographic targeting based on legitimate service areas
- [ ] No discriminatory targeting or content
- [ ] Required disclosures included where needed

### Content Compliance:
- [ ] Headlines don't make prohibited claims
- [ ] Descriptions include all required information
- [ ] Call-to-action language appropriate
- [ ] Business information accurate and current
- [ ] Contact information verified

### Technical Compliance:
- [ ] Landing page complies with Google policies
- [ ] Conversion tracking doesn't violate privacy rules
- [ ] Ad extensions follow formatting rules
- [ ] Keyword matching doesn't trigger policy violations

## POLICY VIOLATION RESPONSE PROCEDURES

### If Ads Disapproved:
1. **Review disapproval reason** in Google Ads
2. **Edit ad content** to comply
3. **Resubmit for approval**
4. **Document changes** for future reference
5. **Monitor approval status**

### Common Disapproval Reasons:
- **Destination not working**: Fix landing page
- **Text doesn't match destination**: Align ad text with landing page
- **Prohibited content**: Remove violating terms
- **Editorial issues**: Fix grammar/spelling
- **Policy violations**: Address specific policy

### Prevention Measures:
- [ ] Regular policy reviews before major changes
- [ ] Test ad variations for approval
- [ ] Monitor disapproval rates weekly
- [ ] Keep policy documentation current
- [ ] Train on policy requirements

## POLICY VIOLATION ESCALATION

### Minor Violations:
- Fix immediately
- Document resolution
- Monitor for recurrence

### Major Violations:
- Pause affected campaigns
- Contact Google Ads support if needed
- Implement corrective action plan
- Prevent future occurrences

### Account at Risk:
- Immediate campaign suspension
- Full account audit
- Compliance training required
- Enhanced monitoring implemented
