# Wright's Impact Window & Door - Client Ad Copy Requirements

## Overview

This document contains **MANDATORY client-specific ad copy requirements** that must be included in 100% of campaigns. These are direct requests from Wright's Impact Window & Door and represent their core messaging priorities.

**CRITICAL**: Client requests must be included intuitively but completely. They do not need to be verbatim word-for-word, but the core concepts, benefits, and positioning must be present in every ad variation.

## Core Value Propositions (MANDATORY - All Must Be Included)

### Energy Efficiency Benefits
- **Client Request**: "Improve Energy Efficiency"
- **Required Inclusion**: Must appear in energy-focused campaigns and asset groups
- **Intuitive Integration**: "Reduce energy costs with superior efficiency" or "Energy-efficient windows that cut utility bills"

- **Required Inclusion**: Must appear in all residential campaigns

### Insurance Benefits
- **Client Request**: "Lower Insurance Rates"
- **Required Inclusion**: Must appear in hurricane protection campaigns
- **Intuitive Integration**: "Insurance discounts with certified protection" or "Lower premiums with impact windows"

### Property Value Increase
- **Client Request**: "Improve Property Value"
- **Required Inclusion**: Must appear in all campaigns targeting homeowners

### Home Protection Messaging

## Implementation Requirements

### Campaign Coverage
- **100% Inclusion Rate**: Every campaign, ad group, and asset group must include at least one client request element
- **Distribution Strategy**: Spread client requests across different ad variations to maximize reach
- **Priority Mapping**:
  - Hurricane Protection campaigns: Focus on security, insurance, and safeguarding
  - Energy Efficiency campaigns: Focus on energy savings and property value
  - Commercial campaigns: Focus on security and property value
  - Financing campaigns: Focus on PACE financing details

### Technical Writing Integration
- **Character Limits**: Client requests must fit within technical writing standards (22-29 chars headlines, 75-85 chars descriptions)
- **Value Density**: Integrate client requests without adding filler words
- **Jam-Packing**: Combine client requests with strategic elements for maximum impact

### Validation Requirements
- **Automated Checking**: System must verify client requests are present in final ad copy
- **Intuitive Detection**: AI must recognize when client concepts are included (not just exact phrases)
- **Coverage Reports**: Generate reports showing which client requests are included where
