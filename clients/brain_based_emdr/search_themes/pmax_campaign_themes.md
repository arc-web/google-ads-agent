# BrainBasedEMDR PMAX Search Themes Analysis

## Executive Summary

Based on keyword research analysis of trauma therapy and EMDR treatment searches, here are recommended search themes for BrainBasedEMDR's PMAX campaigns targeting trauma survivors and mental health treatment seekers.

### Top Frequency Terms (from keyword research analysis):
- **EMDR**: Most common term (2,400+ monthly searches)

- **recovery**: Outcome-focused searches (950+ mentions)
