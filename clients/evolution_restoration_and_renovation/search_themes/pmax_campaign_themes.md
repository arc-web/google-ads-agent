# EvoRestore Performance Max Campaign Themes

## Campaign Structure Overview

EvoRestore's Performance Max campaigns are organized around core service areas with emergency response positioning.

## Emergency Services Campaign
**Primary Focus**: 24/7 emergency response and immediate service needs
**Key Themes**:
- Emergency water damage restoration
- 24/7 flood cleanup services
- Urgent water extraction
- Emergency restoration response
- Flood damage emergency services

## Water Damage Restoration Campaign
**Primary Focus**: Professional water damage mitigation and restoration
**Key Themes**:
- Water damage restoration services
- Professional water cleanup
- Basement water damage repair
- Water extraction and drying
- Structural drying services

## Fire & Smoke Damage Campaign
**Primary Focus**: Fire damage cleanup and restoration
**Key Themes**:
- Fire damage restoration
- Smoke damage cleanup
- Soot removal services
- Fire restoration company
- Professional fire damage repair

## Mold Remediation Campaign
**Primary Focus**: Mold inspection, removal, and prevention
**Key Themes**:
- Mold remediation services
- Professional mold removal
- Toxic mold cleanup
- Mold inspection services
- Mold damage restoration

## Storm Damage Campaign
**Primary Focus**: Weather-related damage restoration
**Key Themes**:
- Storm damage restoration
- Hurricane cleanup services
- Wind damage repair
- Roof damage restoration
- Emergency storm response

## Content Restoration Campaign
**Primary Focus**: Document and personal property restoration
**Key Themes**:
- Document restoration services
- Content cleaning and restoration
- Fire damaged document recovery
- Water damaged content restoration
- Professional document restoration

## Commercial Property Campaign
**Primary Focus**: Business property restoration services
**Key Themes**:
- Commercial restoration services
- Business property damage
- Office building restoration
- Commercial fire damage
- Business interruption restoration

## Residential Services Campaign
**Primary Focus**: Home restoration and emergency services
**Key Themes**:
- Residential restoration services
- Home water damage repair
- House fire restoration
- Emergency home services
- Residential mold remediation

## Asset Group Strategy

Each campaign contains asset groups tailored to specific service scenarios:
- Emergency response scenarios
- Property type targeting (residential vs commercial)
- Damage type specialization
- Geographic service areas
- Seasonal service needs

## Call-to-Action Focus

Primary CTAs emphasize urgency and professionalism:
- "Call Now for Emergency Service"
- "24/7 Emergency Response"
- "Licensed & Insured Technicians"
- "Free Emergency Inspection"
- "Professional Restoration Services"

## Targeting Strategy

- Location-based targeting for service areas
- Emergency service intent keywords
- Property damage awareness campaigns
- Local service area dominance
- Trust and certification signals
